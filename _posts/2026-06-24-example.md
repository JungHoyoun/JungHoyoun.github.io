## Summary

This is an example note for the new Notes section.

The purpose of this page is to check how study notes will look on the site before writing real entries. Future notes can be used for paper summaries, implementation learnings, debugging records, benchmark observations, and research ideas.

## Template

When writing a study note, I will usually keep the shape simple:

- What I studied
- Why it matters
- Key ideas
- Things I misunderstood
- Follow-up questions

## Example Topic

Suppose I studied GPU memory bandwidth bottlenecks in LLM training. A useful note would not just repeat definitions. It should capture the practical lesson:

> If the operation is memory-bound, reducing HBM traffic can matter more than reducing arithmetic.

That kind of note is useful later because it connects the concept to real training-system decisions.
